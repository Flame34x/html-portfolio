Grey's Favorite Movies

Top 3 Movies

Star Wars Episode 3: Revenge of the Sith

I am completely blinded by childhood nostolgia when I think of this movie. To the lightsaber fights, the drama, the poor acting, and even the betrayal, this movie still is one of my favorite to date. Rating: 8/10

Real Steel

Real Steel came out in 2010 and completely blinded me to any other sci-fi movies for at least 4 months. Boxing + robots? How could you make a better combo? Rating: 9/10

Fury

I'm a sucker for World War 2 films. Including some of my favorite actors from other projects comes the true American Hero story told the best way it could've ever been told. "ON THE WAY!" Rating: 10/10
